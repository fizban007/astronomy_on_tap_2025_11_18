What are Pulsars?

Almost immediately after the discovery of pulsars, physicists postulate that they are rapidly rotating neutron stars

How do Pulsars Shine?

Pulsars are surrounded by a lot of plasma, and the radiation we see is powered by the plasma interacting with the magnetic field.
